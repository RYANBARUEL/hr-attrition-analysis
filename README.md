# HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview
The **HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze employee attrition trends, workforce metrics, and key drivers affecting employee turnover. The dashboard provides HR teams and decision-makers with insights to support retention strategies and workforce planning.

## Key Metrics (KPIs)

- Total Employees: **1,470**

- Attrition Count: **237**
- Attrition Rate: **16.12%**
- Average Tenure: **7 Years**
  
---

## Dashboard Features

### Workforce KPI Monitoring
Track workforce performance using:
- Total Employees
- Active Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate
- Average Employee Tenure

### Attrition Trend Analysis
Analyze employee turnover trends over time to identify periods with increased attrition.

### Department-Level Analysis
Identify departments with the highest employee turnover rates.

### Overtime Impact Analysis
Evaluate the relationship between overtime and employee attrition.

### Employee Segmentation
Analyze attrition by:
- Age Band
- Tenure Band
- Job Role

### High-Risk Role Identification
Identify job positions with the highest attrition rates.

### Business Insights & Recommendations
Generate actionable recommendations to support employee retention initiatives.

---

## Business Insights

- Overall attrition rate is **16.12%** with **237 employees leaving**.
- Sales department recorded the highest attrition rate (**20.63%**).
- Employees working overtime showed higher attrition rates.
- Employees aged **25–34** and those with **1–2 years tenure** experienced higher turnover.
- High-turnover roles include **Laboratory Technician** and **Sales Executive**.
