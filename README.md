# HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data to identify key factors influencing workforce turnover.  
An interactive **Power BI dashboard** was built to support HR decision-making and improve employee retention strategies.

---

## Key KPIs

- Total Employees  
- Attrition Count  
- Attrition Rate (%)  
- Active Employees  
- Average Years at Company  

---

## Dashboard Highlights

- Attrition Rate by Overtime (Column Chart)  
- Attrition by Tenure Band (Clustered Column Chart)  
- Attrition Rate by Department (Bar Chart)  
- Attrition Count by Age Band (Line Chart)  
- Top 10 Job Roles by Attrition Count  

- Interactive slicers for Department, Business Travel, Over Time, and Job Role

## Tools Used

- **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX measures, and visualization  
- **Excel / CSV** – Data cleaning and preparation  

---

## Key Insights

- Employees working overtime show higher attrition rates.
- Attrition is more common among employees with shorter tenure.
- Certain departments and job roles experience consistently higher attrition.
- Age and tenure trends provide clear signals for targeted retention strategies.
